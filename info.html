About Red Pandas

Red pandas are small mammals native to the eastern Himalayas and southwestern China. They are known for their reddish-brown fur, bushy tails, and adorable faces.

They are mostly active at dawn and dusk, feeding primarily on bamboo, but they also eat fruits, berries, and small insects.

Red pandas are excellent climbers and spend most of their time in trees. Their bushy tails help with balance and keeping warm in cold weather.
